Overview

Purpose

My website will revolve around my services of building custom computers for people. I already have a big portfolio of PCs that I have built for people so I will describe and show the type of PCs I could build for different customers depending on their desires and budget.

Audience

My audience will be to any person of any age that wants to play video games on PCs. The site will easily explain the purpose of each computer and price range so anyone with a basic understanding of video games will understand the data.
