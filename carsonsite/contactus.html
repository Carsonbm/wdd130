Frequently Asked Questions

Before reaching out to us, you might find your answer faster in our FAQ section.

I just plugged in my PC, and it will not turn on?

Try flipping the switch on the power supply right by the power cord.

The monitor will not turn on?

Ensure that the HDMI or Display Port cable is plugged into the graphics card (GPU) instead of the motherboard by all your USB plugs.
